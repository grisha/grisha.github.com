After nearly two years of hacking, I am tagging this version of Tgres as beta. It is functional and stable enough for people to try out and not feel like they are wasting their time. There is still a lot that could and should be improved, but at this point the most important thing is to get more people to check it out. What is Tgres? Tgres is a Go program which can receive time series data via Graphite, Statsd protocols or an http pixel, store it in PostgreSQL, and provide Graphite-like access to the data in a way that is compatible with tools such as Grafana.
